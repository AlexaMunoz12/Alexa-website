Projects.

➤ VirtualMentor_

Virtual Mentor is a collaborative project built by a team of four to help product managers effectively integrate AI into their workflows. The tool enables PMs to generate feature ideas, summarize requirements, and make faster decisions, with usability testing and feedback leading to a 40% reduction in task completion time and improved navigation clarity.

➤ Crave _

Crave is a full-stack web platform created by three college students to help students eat healthier by simplifying meal planning. The app delivers personalized recipe recommendations and nearby grocery options, with user-driven improvements leading to higher engagement, faster meal planning, and increased repeat usage.

➤ 2048 Game _

I recreated the game 2048 from scratch by building the full game logic and user interface. The game allows players to combine numbered tiles through keyboard controls, handling movement, merging rules, score tracking, and game-over conditions.
